**What is software development?**

At it's core its the process of creating, designing, implementing, and maintaining software applications or systems. You're crafting solutions to real world problems with the help of cool computer programming languages. 

You architect your software, break down problems into small chunks to reduce complexity. You need to solve problems that the user might face in terms of usability and comfort. 

Apart from creating you will also find yourself working on finding bugs and collaborating with your team. The tech field is always improving so be open to continous learning and have a growth mindset. 

Learn smart version control usage and get good at documenting your projects. 


**What does a software developer do?**

Major part of your day is obviously writing code, but you'll be also fixing the bugs thar are bugging you, you'll optimize existing code. But, this role is not alone, you collaborate with people to solve problems. Reiview eachothers code and try to come up with the best solution. Test, Test, Test and Retest. You will always need to spend time to spend time to catch up with the trending languages/frameworks. 

Documentation is a really crucial skill, for you and others.
